Where reality dissolves into dreams

Jacek Błoński is a Polish painter whose work transcends the boundaries between the conscious and subconscious mind. Through layers of abstraction and surrealist imagery, his paintings invite viewers into worlds where the familiar becomes strange and the impossible becomes tangible.

Working primarily with oil and acrylic, Błoński creates canvases that pulse with hidden meaning and emotional depth. Each painting is a portal—a meditation on the nature of perception and the landscapes that exist just beyond the edge of waking life.

His work has been exhibited across Poland and Europe, resonating with collectors who seek art that challenges, provokes, and ultimately transforms.

— Jacek Błoński
